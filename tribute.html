Lata Mangeshkar - The Nightingale Of India

About the Nightingale Of India

☛ Lata Mangeshkar is also famously called the “Nightingale of Bollywood” or “Swar Kokila.”

☛ She was born on 28th September 1929 in Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

☛ She was just 13 years old when her father passed away, and being the eldest of all the siblings, all the family’s responsibilities fell on her shoulders.

☛ Lataji started her singing career in 1942 with the song “Naachu Yaa” for a Marathi movie.

☛ She has sung around 50,000 songs in her entire lifetime.

☛ Lata Mangeshkar’s name is recorded in the Guinness Book for singing the most number of songs in the world.

☛ Lataji has been honoured with many prestigious awards, including the Bharat Ratna.

☛ She breathed her last in Mumbai’s Breach Candy Hospital on 6th February, 2022.
